Portfolio

The projects below are part of my learning experiances, most of them are completed during online studies and some are my personal interest. Check them out and let me know what you think.

Investigate Dataset

In this project, medical appointment data was analyzed, emphasis was given to finding out the factors that are important in predicting if a patient will show up for the appointment or not

Analyze A/B Test Result

In this project, The result of an A/B test run by an e-commerce website was analyzed. The goal was to work through the test to help the company understand if they should implement the new page, keep the old page, or perhaps run the experiment longer to make their decision.

Data Wrangling

In this project, the concepts learned from Udacity Data Wrangling was applied to gather data from a variety of sources and in a variety of formats, assess its quality and tidiness, then clean it,analyze and visualize the result.

Data Visualization

The information regarding 6028 houses from 3 different neighborhoods were explored in this project, the explored and visualized data include price, number of bedrooms, number of bathrooms and style of the house.

Image Classifier

The project classifies pet images using a pretrained CNN model, compares these classifications to the true identity of the pets in the images, and summarizes how well the CNN performed on the image classification task. Note that the true identity of the pet (or object) in the image is indicated by the filename of the image.
