Structure of the Data set¶

The dataset contains 6028 house entries, with seven (7) features (house_id, neighborhood, area, bedrooms, bathrooms, style and price). Most variables not numeric in nature, the variables neighborhood and style of the house are string.

Feature(s) of interest¶

The features I am interested most are those features best for predicting price price of the house in the dataset. I expect that area, bedrooms and bathrooms will have strong effect on the prediction, the bigger the area the higher the number of bedrooms and bathrooms as well as the price of the house, also, as the number of bedrooms and bathrooms increase, the price should increase. I also think that the neighborhood and the house style should have effect on the price.

Conclusion¶

The extended investigation of price neighborhood and style in this section showed that there indeed a positive effect of increased price of houses. The neighborhood has more effect on price than area as seen above as houses of the same size turn out to be cheaper in some neighborhood compared to others. The number of bedrooms also has great effect on the price, while the number of bathrooms is not a big deal when it comes to pricing.
